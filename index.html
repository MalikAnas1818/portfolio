About Me

Engineering thought into working systems

I'm Muhammad Anis, a Generative AI Engineer and the founder of MindForge AI. My work sits at the intersection of large language models, autonomous agents and real-world automation — turning raw ideas into systems that reason, decide and act.

I specialize in building end-to-end LLM applications, RAG pipelines, and multi-agent workflows using LangChain and LangGraph, paired with strong foundations in machine learning, deep learning and computer vision. From WhatsApp AI agents to intelligent attendance systems, I enjoy shipping products that feel like the future.

When I'm not training models or designing agent architectures, I'm exploring new research, contributing to open source, and growing MindForge AI as a studio for intelligent products and business automation.
